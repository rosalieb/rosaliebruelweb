Rosalie Bruel


My research interests revolve around how aquatic ecosystems are responding to local and global perturbations. In order to identify and characterize (both quantitatively and qualitatively) major environmental drivers, I work with a pluridisciplinary approach on lowland and high-altitude lakes, at different spatial (local, regional, global) and temporal (millennial to sub-hourly resolution) scales, and on different responses (physical, chemical, biological).

I am a newly hired researcher at the French Biodiversity Agency (OFB). This is my personal research page, and what I have up here does not engage the responsibility of my employer.
